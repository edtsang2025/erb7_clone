How do I make a booking?

To make a booking, simply search for your desired destination, select your dates, choose a hotel, and follow the booking process. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your reservation.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, most bookings can be modified up to 24 hours before check-in. Log into your account, go to "My Bookings," and select the booking you wish to modify. Some rates may have restrictions on changes.

What is the minimum age to book a hotel?

The minimum age to book a hotel through our platform is 18 years old. Some hotels may have higher age requirements, which will be displayed during the booking process.

When will my credit card be charged?

Most hotels charge your credit card at the time of booking. Some may offer pay-at-property options. The payment policy is clearly displayed during the booking process for each hotel.

Are taxes included in the price?

Yes, all mandatory taxes and fees are included in the displayed price. There are no hidden charges. The final price you see during booking is what you'll pay.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and in some cases, digital wallets like PayPal and Apple Pay.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in. However, some hotels may have different policies, which are clearly stated during booking.

How long does it take to receive a refund?

Refunds typically process within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or credit card company. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.

What if I need to cancel due to an emergency?

In case of emergencies, please contact our customer support team immediately. We'll do our best to work with the hotel to find a suitable solution based on their policies.

How do I create an account?

Click on "Register" in the top navigation and fill out the registration form with your details. You'll need to verify your email address to activate your account.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on "Login" and then "Forgot password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account by going to your profile settings. Please note that this action is permanent and will delete all your booking history and personal data.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our 24/7 customer support team through the "Contact Us" form on our website, or by emailing support@internationalhotels.com. For urgent matters, please call our hotline.

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Do you provide support in multiple languages?

Yes, our customer support team can assist you in English, Spanish, French, German, and Mandarin Chinese.
